**Today I'm 36 years old.**
I’m still living in an apartment with a woman — perhaps as a way to escape loneliness, or maybe in hope of a better future. I’m not entirely sure.

But within one or two years, I want to live like this:

- I have a job involving **Web3D**, where I **lead or contribute** to a meaningful, valuable project.
- I live in a **loft-style apartment**, either alone or with a woman who is independent and able to live her own life. We share a peaceful, respectful space.
- I am a **Web3D writer**, publishing **at least one article per week**.
- I’m writing a **book for beginners** about Web3D or Three.js.
- I’m part of a **community** where we share ideas, support each other’s projects, and grow both creatively and financially.
- I have **several new, genuine friends** — regardless of age — who I can talk to, work with, or simply enjoy life with.
- I feel confident speaking to the camera, creating **video courses** for platforms like YouTube or Bilibili.
- I still live in **Kunming**, or another city in Yunnan that brings me peace.

From this day forward, I will work toward this life — no matter what others say, even my closest friends.

**I believe in God.**
